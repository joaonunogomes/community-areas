# Privacy Policy

**9Way Climbing**
**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

## Overview

9Way Climbing is a boulder climbing logbook app that stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

## Data We Collect

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Climbing data:** Area names, boulder problem names, difficulty grades, star ratings, tags, ascent types, attempt counts, and ascent dates.
- **Photos:** Images you select from your photo library for boulders and climbing areas. These are compressed and stored locally on your device.
- **Location data:** Geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) you choose to associate with climbing areas or boulder problems.

## How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. **No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.** There are no user accounts, no analytics, and no telemetry.

## Data Sharing & Export

- You can export your climbing data as a backup file (ZIP archive) using the app's backup feature.
- You can share individual areas or boulders with others using your device's native share functionality.
- These actions are initiated entirely by you. The app never shares your data automatically.

## Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

- **Location (foreground only):** Used to auto-fill coordinates when logging climbing areas or boulders. Location is only accessed when you explicitly use the location picker. The app does not track your location in the background.
- **Photo Library:** Used to let you select images for boulders and climbing areas. The app does not access your photo library without your interaction.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's settings.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Map views use your device's default map provider (Apple Maps on iOS, Google Maps on Android), which are subject to their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and no data is transmitted, the app does not pose data collection risks for users of any age.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. You have full control over your data at all times.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
